Core Premise

Separate speech from cash-out.

Voice Credits throttle how much someone can speak, Trust captures reputation that cannot be sold, and Boost is the only spendable amplifier. That separation blocks bot farms while letting real users cash in on earned trust.

VC
Voice Credits

Oxygen for posting. Regens daily. Refunds when quality lands.

TR
Trust

Non-transferable reputation. Raises caps and unlocks features.

BT
Boost

Spendable spotlight fuel. Earned from Trust or top signal moments.

Refund Logic

If a post is rated helpful, fair, or cited within the refund window, 50–100% of its VC cost is returned. Noise flags increase drift and raise costs.

Rewards Pool

Cash-out only via earned Boost.

BT redemption is rate-limited, KYC'd, and only applies to earned BT. Purchased boosts never cash out. Use cooldowns, audits, and clawbacks to shut down fraud incentives.

KYC + minimum threshold
BT aging window (7–30 days)
Clawback for proven abuse
Abuse Resistance

Make trust expensive to fake.

  • Device + IP risk scoring with verified phone/email.
  • Structured rooms with peer feedback for TR earnings.
  • Weighted ratings: high-TR weights, capped for fairness.
  • New-account throttle: can speak, but can’t earn much yet.
  • Slashing for brigading, harassment, or misinfo fraud.
